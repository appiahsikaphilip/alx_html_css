F.A.Q

How does this work?

SmileSchool provides video-based tutorials taught by professional smile coaches. Simply register for free, browse our catalog of courses, and start learning at your own pace. Each tutorial includes step-by-step instructions, practice exercises, and tips from experts who have won multiple smile awards. You can watch videos on any device and track your progress throughout your learning journey.

Is the membership really free?

Yes! Our basic membership is completely free with no hidden fees or credit card required. You'll get access to our most popular tutorials, community forums, and basic learning materials. We believe everyone deserves to learn the art of smiling. Premium features like advanced courses and one-on-one coaching sessions are available for users who want to take their skills to the next level.

What makes your instructors qualified?

Our instructors are award-winning professionals with years of experience in smile coaching, photography, and personal development. Each instructor has won prestigious awards such as "Smile of the Year" and "Best Little Smile" competitions. They've helped thousands of students improve their confidence and master various smiling techniques. All instructors undergo a rigorous vetting process to ensure the highest quality education.

Can I access courses on mobile devices?

Absolutely! SmileSchool is fully optimized for mobile devices, tablets, and desktop computers. You can learn on the go, whether you're commuting, traveling, or relaxing at home. Our responsive platform ensures a seamless learning experience across all devices. Download the mobile app for offline viewing and push notifications when new courses are added to your favorites.
